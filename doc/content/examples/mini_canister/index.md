# Mini-Canister Transport

TMAP8 is used to model the hydrogen permeation within aluminum-clad used nuclear fuel (AUNF) mini-canister storage devices at Savannah River National Laboratory (SRNL). steel_only.i simulates the hydrogen permeation through only the steel wall, and gas_steel.i simulates the hydrogen generation and transport inside the canister in addition to the permeation through the steel walls.
